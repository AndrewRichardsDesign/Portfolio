Millennial Banking

MIT Design Lab

Extensive research for a Brazilian bank client on millennial social trends and the fintech industry, culminating in a customer and employee attraction strategy and a concept generation process applying research findings.

Context

This research project took just over a year to complete. It consists of three main parts, shown in the sections below. Our goal was to build an understanding of how millennials live and what types of products they use.

Our client was interested in the millennial generation because it was an untapped market segment for them. Our research helped our client understand how they can shape their work environment for millennial employees and design their products to meet the unique needs and ideals of millennials.
