Customize

USAA - Member Home

We offer many customization features to accommodate various mental models and organizational structures we've seen members use and request. This way, member are able to organize their accounts and policies anyway they'd like.

Getting In and Out of Customize Mode

This mode is intended to feel like a transformation of the homepage to create a feeling of direct manipulation. Being an infrequently visited space, we chose to place the entry point at the bottom of the page. Usability testing showed no issues with this placement.

Reorder Accounts, Policies, Groups, and Sections

Changing order is critical feature for organizational freedom. We've made this process seamless with an intuitive drag and drop interaction that allows products to be moved to any group or section, regardless of default categorization. In this way, we truly allow members to arrange their products in a way that makes sense to them.

Edit an Account or Policy

Upon clicking a product, a popover interaction allows members to rename a product, hide the balance, or hide the product itself. Actions in the popover are reflected on the page in real time.

Create, Add To, Delete, and Edit Groups

Our 'groups' feature allows members to further organize their products. This is useful when organizing accounts for a family, for unique purposes, or simply bringing order to a large number of accounts.

Switch Between Grid and List View

We created grid view in an effort to simplify the UI, but there are still traditional-minded members who prefer list view and appreciate seeing an asset and liability total after their accounts.
