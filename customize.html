Customize

Members are able to view their accounts and policies in either grid or list view. These options target member segments looking for a more modern, simple presentation and those looking for a traditional tally view that provides asset and liability totals.

Overview of customization features

Reorder

A member is able to reorder individual products, groups, and entire categories.

Edit account / policy

Within customize mode, a member is able to change the name of a product, hide it from view, and hide balances. Outside of customize mode, members can temporarily show their hidden accounts.

Create, edit, and delete groups

A member can create groups to organize their products.

Toggle grid and list view

Members can easily toggle between the two views that we offer for a simplified or traditional tally view.
