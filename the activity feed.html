The Activity Feed

USAA - Member Home

The activity feed (right column) is designed to provide information and allow members to take actions related to their relationship with USAA. It’s powered by a prioritization algorithm that ranks cards, considering the member’s utilization, product line up, and events like tax season.

Claims activity card

The claims card is one of two activity cards I designed. It provides statuses at each step in the process, an entry point to the claims center, and a message counter from the member’s ongoing conversation with claims adjusters.

"Real estate rewards network" activity card

The real estate rewards network card was intended to walk members through the process of buying and selling a house and ultimately receiving a monetary reward from USAA, but budget constraints simplified the solution to a simple list of properties and a corresponding reward status (pending or sent).
